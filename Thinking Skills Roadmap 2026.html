Decompose &

Daily Practice — Critical
Socratic Questioning — challenge every claim: what's the evidence? assumptions?
Steelman First — build the strongest opposing argument before dismissing it
Fallacy Hunting — spot logical errors in one article or video daily
Source Triangulation — 3 independent primary sources before forming opinions
10-min Reflection — "How could I have thought about this better?"
Daily Practice — Analytical
MECE Drills — break any daily problem into non-overlapping, exhaustive branches
Fermi Questions — estimate anything quantitatively to build number fluency
5 Whys — for any setback, ask why 5 times until you find the root cause
Case Decomposition — map causes & consequences for any news story
Data Literacy — question every graph, chart, or statistic you encounter
Daily Practice — Design
1 Empathy Interview/week — listen to a user's challenge; don't solve, just understand
Observation Journal — spot one poorly-designed thing daily; sketch a fix
HMW Reframing — turn any problem into 5+ "How Might We…" questions
20-min Prototype Sprint — sketch or build any idea under a tight time constraint
SCAMPER Drill — apply all 7 lenses to any product or process in 7 minutes

6-Phase Learning Journey

Approximate timeframes · Adjust to your pace · Revisit phases as you deepen
Week 1–2
Foundation
  • Read definitions (Parts 1–3)
  • Understand the master sequence
  • Start a Thinking Journal
  • Read: Thinking F&S (Kahneman)
Week 3–6
Critical Core
  • Study Paul-Elder Framework
  • Practise Bloom's levels 1–4
  • Daily fallacy & bias hunting
  • Apply RED Model to 5 articles
Week 7–12
Analytical Engine
  • Master MECE & Issue Trees
  • Practise Pyramid Principle in writing
  • Apply 5 Whys to real problems
  • Read: McKinsey Way (Rasiel)
Month 4–5
Design Mindset
  • Run a personal mini Design Sprint
  • Conduct 4 empathy interviews
  • Build & test one prototype
  • Read: Creative Confidence (Kelley)
Month 6–9
Integration
  • Apply all 3 skills to one real project
  • Study Cynefin + Systems Thinking
  • Join a hackathon or case competition
  • Monthly self-evaluation audit
Month 10+
Mastery Loop
  • Teach a framework to someone else
  • Quarterly skills audit (score 1–10)
  • Combine frameworks deliberately
  • Never stop: only a rising standard
