Jeevan Kaushal 2.0 — जीवन कौशल

A complete life skills curriculum for Classes 9–12 built on the Indian Intelligence Stack Architecture. Every module follows IKS-first, story-driven pedagogy: Story → Reflection → Strategy → Simulation → Application. Same stories deepen across 4 tiers (T1–T4) through spiral learning.

📋 Framework Lineage: UGC Jeevan Kaushal 2.0 (Aug 2023) × Indian Intelligence Stack Architecture × NEP 2020. 4 Pillars restructured into 4 Phases. 33 modules compressed into 12-week core + 24-week electives. IKS stories as primary pedagogy across all pillars.
