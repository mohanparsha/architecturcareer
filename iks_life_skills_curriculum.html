Indian Intelligence Stack Architecture

Interactive 12-week life skills curriculum built on the progression of Story → Reflection → Strategy → Simulation → Application. Designed for Classes 9–12 in the Indian context, this guide converts ancient Indian wisdom into a structured decision-intelligence learning system.

📋 Framework Lineage: Adapted from UGC's Curriculum & Guidelines for Life Skills (Jeevan Kaushal) 2.0 (August 2023) under NEP 2020. This curriculum uses Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS) as source material to introduce secondary school students to core life skills through story-driven experiential learning. It serves as a structured starting point aligned with the national vision for holistic, value-based education.

Teaching Method Loop

Use the same 5-step session structure every week for consistency, reflection, and measurable skill growth.
1. StoryAnchor the theme through a culturally rooted IKS narrative.
2. ReflectionSurface meaning, motive, and consequence through guided questions.
3. StrategyExtract decision frameworks and practical life lessons.
4. SimulationPractice the skill through role-play, scenario, or lab.
5. ApplicationConnect learning to school, home, society, and future work.
This interactive guide is structured as a single source of truth for both students and teachers. Each module includes story summaries, assessment rubrics, deliverable templates, session timing, teacher preparation notes, and curated resources.
